Privacy Policy

Just One Job Apps is built around a simple idea: use the smallest amount of data possible to make the app work, and never collect what isn't needed.

Data Collection

Our apps are designed to work entirely in your browser. We do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Store data on our servers
  • Require accounts or sign-ups
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes
  • Share data with third parties

How Our Apps Work

All calculations and data processing happen locally in your browser. Any information you enter (like bill amounts or tip percentages) stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Website Analytics

This website does not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts that would collect information about your visit.

Contact

If you have questions about our privacy practices, you can reach us at hello@justonejobapps.com.
