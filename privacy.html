Privacy Policy

Just One Job Apps LLC
Last updated: November 15, 2025

Just One Job Apps LLC ("we", "us", or "our") creates small, focused applications designed to work quickly and without unnecessary data collection. Our guiding principle is simple: use the smallest amount of data necessary and avoid collecting anything we do not need.

This Privacy Policy describes how our website and apps handle information. It applies to:

  • The website justonejobapps.com
  • Our web apps, including tip.justonejobapps.com
  • Any future apps released under the Just One Job Apps name

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our apps are designed so that we do not collect, store, or share personal information about you. In particular, we do not collect:

  • Names or contact information
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Location or GPS data
  • Contacts, calendars, or photos
  • Advertising identifiers or device identifiers for tracking
  • Analytics or usage profiles tied to you as an individual
  • Payment or financial information

Our apps do not require accounts, logins, sign-ups, or subscriptions. We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking pixels in our apps.

2. Website Hosting and Basic Logs

The website justonejobapps.com is hosted by Netlify. When you visit the site, Netlify may automatically collect standard web server logs, which can include:

  • IP address in server logs
  • Browser type and version
  • Basic device information
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Date and time of the request

This information is handled by Netlify for security, reliability, and basic operational purposes (for example, to prevent abuse and ensure the service runs correctly). We do not use this data to create user profiles, track individual visitors, or run analytics or advertising campaigns.

For more details on Netlify's handling of data, please see their privacy policy: https://www.netlify.com/privacy/.

3. Data Stored on Your Device (Apps Only)

Some of our apps may store small pieces of data locally on your device to make the app more convenient. Examples include:

  • Your last entered bill amount
  • Your last selected tip percentage
  • Simple app settings or preferences

This data:

  • Is stored only on your device or in your browser
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Can usually be cleared by deleting the app, resetting the app data, or clearing your browser data

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or similar technologies for advertising, user tracking, or behavioral profiling.

Our website may use strictly necessary cookies or similar mechanisms required for basic site operation (for example, security or content delivery), but not for analytics or marketing purposes that identify you.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Because we design our apps to avoid collecting personal information, we also do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

We do not use:

  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Marketing or remarketing platforms

6. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are designed to be simple utility tools and do not target children specifically. Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age.

7. Links to Other Sites

Our website or apps may contain links to third-party websites or app stores (for example, the Apple App Store or Google Play). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those third-party sites or services. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external services you visit.

8. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and adjust the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Any future changes will remain aligned with our core principles of minimal data use and clear, simple explanations.

We will not introduce tracking, analytics, or additional data collection without clearly describing it in an updated version of this policy.
